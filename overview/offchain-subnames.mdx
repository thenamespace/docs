## Use Cases

- **Wallets**: Give every user a branded identity (e.g. _user_.**wallet**.eth).
- **Communities**: DAOs or NFT projects issue subnames or assign to NFTs (_alice_.**bayc**.eth).
- **L2s / RaaS providers**: Provide an identity layer for the entire ecosystem (_bob_.**base**.eth).
- **Apps**: Payment, DeFi, or Social apps assign subnames automatically to users (_carol_.**app**.eth).
- **AI Agents**: Assign persistent, verifiable identities to AI agents (_xbtc_.**agent**.eth).

## How to create an offchain subname

1. Go to the [Namespace Dev App](https://dev.namespace.ninja/)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Click on the "Create Subname" button
4. Select the ENS name you want to create a subname for
5. Click on the "Create Subname" button
