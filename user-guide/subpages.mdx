Subpages is a customizable white-label subname minting template that enables ENS name owners (companies, communities, DAOs, and others) to issue subnames from a custom-built website in record time. For example, if an organization owns _awesome.eth_, using Subpages, they can quickly spin up a custom website to allow members to mint personalized subnames like **alice**.awesome.eth.

## **Features**

- **Quick Deployment** — fully functional minting site in minutes
- **Customizable Design** — tailor appearance to your brand
- **Wallet Integration** — RainbowKit out of the box
- **ENS Subname registration** — mint under your ENS name
- **L1 and L2** Compatibility
- **Responsive UI** — works on all devices
- **Referral System** — incentivize sharing
